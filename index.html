Key Galaxy Facts

Our Home 🌟

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy containing 100–400 billion stars, with our Sun about 25,000 light-years from its center.

Types & Structure 🔄

Galaxies are classified as spiral, elliptical, or irregular. The Milky Way is a barred spiral.

Dark Matter 🕳️

Most of a galaxy's mass is invisible dark matter. Only ~5% is visible stars, gas, and dust.

Collision Course 💥

The Milky Way will merge with Andromeda in ~4.5 billion years — forming a new elliptical galaxy.

Sagittarius A* ⚫

Our galactic center hosts Sagittarius A*, a supermassive black hole with 4 million solar masses. In 2022, the Event Horizon Telescope captured its first image.
